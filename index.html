Objective

This meeting aims to introduces students build a foundation of both thinking skills, theoretical knowledge, practical application, and exposure to practical industry-tested experience. Hopefully, this meeting will provide the student a solid foundation for students to continue to build their careers upon. That last point should be internalized, having this meeting will not make you an expert in this topic, significant practice and further study would be required to meet such a goal. This meeting is but the start of a career-long journey for many students.
